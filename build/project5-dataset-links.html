Dataset Links

All free. Aswath Damodaran's industry datasets share one 94-industry key and live at /pc/datasets/<stem>.xls — the datafile HTML pages mislabel their download links, so these point at the canonical files the pipeline actually uses. Click a blue label; raw URL beneath for copy-paste.

Core — the three thesis datasets (Damodaran, NYU Stern)
1 · Operating & Net Margins
Reported and R&D/lease-adjusted operating margins + R&D/Sales, SBC/Sales — the distortion is measured here.
margin.xls (data)https://pages.stern.nyu.edu/~adamodar/pc/datasets/margin.xls
margin.html (definitions)https://pages.stern.nyu.edu/~adamodar/New_Home_Page/datafile/margin.html
2 · R&D Expenditures
R&D intensity, 5-yr R&D, and Damodaran's capitalized-R&D estimate (never paired with the adjusted margin — that is his identity).
R&D.xls (data)https://pages.stern.nyu.edu/~adamodar/pc/datasets/R&D.xls
R&D.html (definitions)https://pages.stern.nyu.edu/~adamodar/New_Home_Page/datafile/R&D.html
The R&D.html "download" link mistakenly points to roe.xls — use the direct R&D.xls URL above.
3 · Financing Flows by Sector
Dividends, buybacks, equity issuance, debt raised/repaid — the funding & payout signature.
finflows.xls (data)https://pages.stern.nyu.edu/~adamodar/pc/datasets/finflows.xls
finflows.html (definitions)https://pages.stern.nyu.edu/~adamodar/New_Home_Page/datafile/finflows.html
Net Equity Change = Issuance − Buybacks (dividends NOT subtracted) — verified against the data; the file's own note is wrong.
Valuation payoff — does the market price the adjusted margin?
4 · Price/Sales & EV/Sales · PE & PEG · Dividends vs FCFE
The market-outcome variables the thesis regresses on (EV/Sales, P/E), plus payout capacity.
psdata.xls — Price/Sales & EV/Saleshttps://pages.stern.nyu.edu/~adamodar/pc/datasets/psdata.xls
pedata.xls — PE, PEG, expected growthhttps://pages.stern.nyu.edu/~adamodar/pc/datasets/pedata.xls
divfcfe.xls — Dividends vs FCFEhttps://pages.stern.nyu.edu/~adamodar/pc/datasets/divfcfe.xls
Index, archives & sibling facts (same 94-industry key)
5 · Data index & the multi-year panel
The catalog of ~30 industry datasets, and the archive that turns a single January snapshot into a panel.
